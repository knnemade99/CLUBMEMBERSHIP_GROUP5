About Club

The mission of The Clud de Amigos is to enable Rotarians to advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through the improvement of health. The Rotary Foundation helps fund our humanitarian activities, from local service projects to global initiatives. Rotarians and friends of Rotary support the Foundation work through voluntary contributions.

Our Prime Offerings

Temporary Members 3 Months

Avail the basic sports facilities offered by the Club.Any individual interested in availing the basic sports facilities offered by the Club can do so by applying for Temporary Membership.

Permanent Members 1 Year

Permanent Membership is an Individual membership.A permanent member can avail any of the facilities offered by the Club on payment of nominal annual fees.

Indoor Games Department

TableTennis, LawnTennis, Chess, Carrom, Swimming are some of the indoor facilities which is been entertained in our club.

Outdoor Games Department

Football, BasketBall, Cricket, Hockey, Horse Riding are some of the outdoor facilities which is been entertained in our club.

Leisure Department

CardRoom, Library, Banquet, Restaurant & Bar are the leisure activities of our club . As the name suggest these activities are just for fun .
